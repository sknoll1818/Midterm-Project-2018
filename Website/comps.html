FAQ

Q:Am I fast enough to go to a competition?

A:Your speed should not be a factor in your decision. If you think you may want to go to a competition, go. nobody will care about your speed, or your mains, or your method, or anything.

Q:What do I need to know before my first competition?

A: You can watch this video here for all of the necessary information.

Q: How many cubes should I bring?

A: Don't bring your whole collection, bring your mains and backup mains if possible. There may be a few cubes that you aren't competing in that you know you want to bring snd solve.

Q: Is competing difficult or stressful?

A: If you attend the competition tutorials and watch competitions from home, you should be good.

Q: What if I can't talk to people?

A: Just walk in, sit down, and ask a few people about their mains and averages and other cubing stuff. Try to be social and you'll make new friends!
