FAQ

Q:Am I fast enough to go to a competition?

A:Your speed should not be a factor in your decision. If you think you may want to go to a competition, go. nobody will care about your speed, or your mains, or your method, or anything.

Q:What do I need to know before my first competition?

A: You can watch this video here for all of the necessary information.

Q: How many cubes should I bring?

A: Don't bring your whole collection, bring your mains and backup mains if possible. There may be a few cubes that you aren't competing in that you know you want to bring snd solve; you should also bring those.

Q: Is competing difficult or stressful?

A: Competing is only stressful if you put pressure on yourself. For a first competition, you have no reason to put stress on yourself. Instead, focus on the people. If you don't get the results you wanted, you have many more oppurtunities.

Q: What if I can't talk to people?

A: That is a normal thing to worry about. Don't be afraid to talk to people. Odds are, they will be friendly, and invite you in to their friend group.
