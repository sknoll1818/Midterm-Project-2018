Events

3x3

For 3x3, I am dual-color-neutral for white/yellow. After that, i build all of my f2l pairs efficiently up until last slot. When I do last slot, if my pieces pair up, I will solve EO unless it requires too many moves. If they do not pair up, I do a normal insert. If EO is solved, I will either use a COLL or ZBLL algorithm; if EO is not solved, I will do OLL then PLL. Here is the ZBLL Doc that I use and the COLL algs I use.

4x4

For 4x4, I use the Yau Method. If you want to learn Yau, Click Here! I only solve white cross on 4x4, so I will start with the white or yellow centers. I will solve cross edges on left, so the next 2 steps are 2-gen. I solve L4C one at a time in a random order. For L8E, I use 3-2-3 pairing; which you can learn here . I use Drew Parity OLL alg and the normal pll parity alg.

5x5/6x6/7x7

For the rest of the big cubes, I use the Reduction Method with Freeslice Edge Grouping. I do color neutral centers, and I do at most one commutator for L2C. I mainly build bars, but sometimes I'll follow different structures. For edges, I group on E and put them into either U or D layer. For L4E, I use slice-flip-slice and I know full L2E for 5x5.

3BLD

I use a white top green front orientation and a rotated Speffz scheme. I follow an ABCD top EFGH front IJKL Right MNOP back QRST left UVWX bottom. My buffers are UF/UFR. I learned most of my comms by drilling them, and they are mostly intuitive. For orozco, I just learned all of the cases, and their inverses as algs instead of comms. For the solve, I follow ECEC and for parity, I setup to Jb Permutation. Once finished, my 3Style trainer will be available here!
